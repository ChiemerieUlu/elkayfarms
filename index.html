ABOUT US

El-kay Farms & Garden is a privately owned indigenous Farm company located at the heart of Bauchi Local Government Area in Bauchi State Nigeria. The company was established with the sole aim of generating economic activities, capacity building in agriculture, job creation, poverty eradication and revenue generation. We commenced farming business over a decade now, however, the company was incorporated by the Corporate Affairs Commission with RC number 7927537 in September 2024.
